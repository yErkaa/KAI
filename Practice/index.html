About Kyrie Irving

Kyrie Irving is the best dribbler in NBA history. He is one of the most skilled basketball players. He is famous for his excellent ball sense, ability to complete the throw and bring points. Kyrie Irving was born on March 23, 1992, in Melbourne, Australia. He played college basketball for the Duke Blue Devils before joining the NBA.
